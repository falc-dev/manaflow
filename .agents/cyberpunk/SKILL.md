## 1. Game Objective & Winning Conditions

### Primary Objective
Collect 6 Gig Dice to win, not reduce Life to zero like traditional TCGs.

### Winning the Game
1. **Momentum Win:** Start your turn with **6 Gig Dice** in your Gig Area
2. **Instant Win:** Reach **7 Gig Dice** at any time

### Why This is Unique
- Gig Dice represent completed jobs/contracts
- Game ends based on dice quantity, not values
- Two small gigs worth more than one big gig

### How Gigs Work
- Gigs are represented by dice (d4 to d20)
- Roll dice from Fixer Area to Gig Area
- Number of dice matters, not face value

## 2. Card Types

### Legends
- Face-down cards placed during setup
- Pay to Call (reveal) them into play
- Powerful characters like V, Johnny Silverhand, T-Bug, Goro Takemura
- Add tension and surprise
- Usually key to strategy

### Units
- Main cards for attacking and blocking
- Have Power values
- Attack to steal Gigs from opponent
- **Power Scaling:** For every 10 Power, steal extra Gig

### Gear
- Attach to Units
- Modify stats (Power, defense)
- Provide utility effects
- Can be moved between Units

### Programs
- One-time tactical plays
- Like "instants" in other games
- Quick hacks, ambushes, reversals
- Can change combat outcomes

### Cyberware
- Type of equipment
- Provides bonuses to Units
- Various effects and synergies

**Source:** https://www.creativegamelife.com/cyberpunk-tcg-alpha-rules-how-to-play-guide

## 3. Game Areas

### Legends Area
- Place 3 Legends face-down during setup
- Hidden from opponent until revealed
- Called one per turn

### Fixer Area
- Where dice are stored before rolling
- Starting die: d4
- Contains available Gig dice

### Gig Area
- Where rolled dice go
- Determines victory (6 to win)
- Target for opponent attacks

### Deck Zone
- Draw pile for cards

### Hand
- Cards available to play
- Maximum hand size: 7 cards

### Eddie Zone
- Currency for purchases
- Starting: 3 Eddies per turn
- Can sell cards for additional Eddies

### Board/Field
- Where Units and Gear are placed

### Discard/Trash
- Used cards go here

**Source:** https://www.creativegamelife.com/cyberpunk-tcg-alpha-rules-how-to-play-guide

## 4. Resource System: Eddies & Dice

### Eddies (Currency)
- Used to pay for cards
- **Starting per turn:** 3 Eddies
- **Starting game:** 5 cards, 3 Eddies

### Selling Cards (Burn Economy)
- Once per turn, sell any card from hand
- Gain Eddies equal to card's value (usually 1)
- Every card has dual value: gameplay + selling
- Creates tough decisions

### Gig Dice
- **d4:** Smallest gigs
- **Larger dice:** Bigger potential
- **Number matters, not value:** Two d4s > one d8

### Die Progression
- Start with d4 in Fixer Area
- Roll to Gig Area each turn
- Larger dice available for larger jobs

**Source:** https://www.creativegamelife.com/cyberpunk-tcg-alpha-rules-how-to-play-guide

## 5. Turn Structure

### Start Phase
1. Check win condition
2. Gain 3 Eddies
3. Draw 1 card
4. Move next die from Fixer to Gig Area

### Action Phase
- Spend Eddies to play Units/Gear
- Call (reveal) one Legend per turn
- Build your board

### Deploy Gigs
- Roll die from Fixer Area
- Move to Gig Area
- Adds to your Gig count

### Combat Phase
- Attack to steal Gigs
- Block to defend
- **10+ Power:** Steal 2 Gigs instead of 1
- Combat is frequent and impactful

### End Phase
- Discard down to 7 cards
- Refresh Units (untap)
- Pass to opponent

**Source:** https://www.creativegamelife.com/cyberpunk-tcg-alpha-rules-how-to-play-guide

## 6. Combat System

### Attacking
- Declare attack on opponent's Gig Area
- Attacker selects target
- Compare Power values

### Power & Gig Theft
- Win: Steal 1 Gig from opponent
- **10+ Power:** Steal 2 Gigs instead
- This "breakpoint" is crucial for strategy

### Blocking
- Spend Blocker to redirect attack
- Armored Units can block effectively
- Defense is not optional

### Combat Resolution
- Winner steals Gigs
- Loser loses Gigs
- Board state changes quickly

### Important Mechanics
- Can attack spent (tapped) units (e.g., Sandevistan)
- Exhaustion doesn't guarantee safety
- Every round is tug of war

**Source:** https://www.creativegamelife.com/cyberpunk-tcg-alpha-rules-how-to-play-guide

## 9. Alpha Kit Rules (Current)

### Setup
- Draw 5 cards
- Start with 3 Eddies
- Place 3 Legends face-down
- Place d4 in Fixer Area

### Win Conditions
- Start turn with 6 Gig Dice = Win
- Reach 7 Gig Dice anytime = Instant Win

### Turn Summary
- Gain 3 Eddies, Draw 1 card
- Play cards (Units/Gear/Call Legend)
- Deploy Gigs (roll die)
- Combat (steal Gigs)
- Discard to 7

### Key Differences from TCGs
- No Life points
- Dice-based victory
- Selling cards for currency
- Different combat timing

## 10. Unique Mechanics

### The "Burn" Economy
- Sell card from hand for Eddies
- Every card has two values
- Strategic sacrifice needed
- Feels like Night City survival

### Information Hiding
- Legends face-down
- Surprises in combat
- Card reading important

### Power Breakpoints
- 10 Power = 2 Gigs stolen
- Build toward this threshold
- High-risk, high-reward

### Tempo vs. Value
- Play now or save for later
- Sell or keep
- Constant pressure
