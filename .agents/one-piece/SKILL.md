## 1. Game Objective & Winning Conditions

### Primary Objective
Reduce your opponent's Leader's Life to zero.

### Starting Conditions
- **Deck:** 50 cards
- **DON!! Deck:** 10 cards
- **Starting Hand:** 5 cards
- **Life Cards:** Equal to Leader's Life value (usually 5-7)
- **First Turn:** Draw phase is skipped

### Winning the Game
1. **Life Reduction:** Reduce opponent's Leader Life to 0
2. **Deck Exhaustion:** Opponent cannot draw a card when required

### Life Card Mechanic
- When your Leader takes damage, place Life cards in your hand
- Life cards can be played as resources
- Creates comeback potential

## 2. Card Types

### Leader Cards
- Represents your crew's captain
- Has unique abilities and Life points
- Must share color with all cards in deck
- Controls the deck's strategy
- Can attack when rested

### Character Cards
- Crew members you play to the field
- Have Power and Cost values
- Can attack and block
- Various types and abilities
- Enter field in rested (horizontal) position

### Event Cards
- One-time effects
- Powerful tactical plays
- Go to trash after resolution
- Can be used during various phases

### Stage Cards
- Permanent locations
- Stay in play until destroyed
- Provide ongoing effects
- Only one Stage per player at a time

### DON!! Cards
- Universal resource cards
- Used to pay costs
- Can be attached to boost Power (+1000 per DON!!)
- 10 cards in your DON!! deck

**Source:** https://tcg-central.com/en/articles/how-to-play-one-piece-tcg-complete-rules-guide-2025.php

## 3. Game Zones

### Leader Zone
- Where your Leader card sits
- Represents your Life total

### Character Zone
- Where your active Characters are
- Maximum varies by format
- Characters can rest/stand

### DON!! Zone
- Storage for DON!! cards
- Cards ready to use

### Cost Zone
- DON!! cards used for payment
- Discarded after use

### Life Zone
- Face-down Life cards
- Represent Leader's health

### Trash Zone
- Used/discarded cards
- Event cards go here after use

### Deck Zones
- Main Deck (50 cards)
- DON!! Deck (10 cards)

**Source:** https://tcg-central.com/en/articles/how-to-play-one-piece-tcg-complete-rules-guide-2025.php

## 4. Card Colors & Synergies

### Color Types
- **Red:** Aggressive, direct damage, aggressive plays
- **Blue:** Hand control, bottom-decking, bounce effects
- **Green:** Resting mechanics, DON!! manipulation
- **Purple:** DON!! manipulation, cost reduction
- **Black:** Cost reduction, character removal, board control
- **Yellow:** Life control, manipulation

### Color Restrictions
- All cards must share color with Leader
- Some cards may be multi-color
- Color determines deck identity

### Deck Building
- Maximum 4 copies of uniquely numbered card
- Must match Leader's colors
- 50-card minimum

**Source:** https://www.reddit.com/r/OnePieceTCG/comments/1i90lsh/card_color_play_styles/

## 5. Turn Structure (5 Phases)

### Phase 1: Refresh Phase
- Activate all rested cards
- Return DON!! to active position
- Reset your resources

### Phase 2: Draw Phase
- Draw 1 card
- First player skips on first turn

### Phase 3: DON!! Phase
- Add DON!! to your Cost Zone
- First turn: 1 DON!!
- Subsequent turns: 2 DON!!

### Phase 4: Main Phase
- Play Character, Event, or Stage cards
- Pay costs with DON!!
- Attach DON!! to boost Power (+1000 each)
- Declare attacks with rested Leaders/Characters

### Phase 5: End Phase
- Resolve end-of-turn effects
- Pass turn to opponent

**Source:** https://tcg-central.com/en/articles/how-to-play-one-piece-tcg-complete-rules-guide-2025.php

## 6. Combat System

### Active/Rest System
- **Active (vertical):** Ready to use
- **Rest (horizontal):** Exhausted after attacking/using

### Attacking
1. Rest your attacker (Leader or Character)
2. Declare target (opposing Leader or rested Character)
3. Opponent may block with rested Characters
4. Compare Power values
5. Higher Power wins, lower takes damage

### Blocking
- Rested Characters can block attacking Characters
- Cannot block Leader attacks (usually)
- Multiple blockers possible (varies)

### Power Comparison
- Character with higher Power wins
- Winner deals damage equal to difference
- Damage reduces HP or goes to Life

### DON!! Boost
- Attach DON!! to increase Power by +1000 each
- Trade resource for attack power
- Key tactical decision

**Source:** https://tcg-central.com/en/articles/how-to-play-one-piece-tcg-complete-rules-guide-2025.php

## 7. Cost & Resource Management

### DON!! Costs
- Each card has a DON!! cost
- Pay by moving DON!! from Cost Zone to Trash
- Must have sufficient DON!! to play card

### DON!! Attachment
- Can attach DON!! to Leader or Characters
- +1000 Power per attached DON!!
- Attached DON!! remain until used or removed

### Life as Resource
- Life cards taken as damage can be played
- Convert defense into offense
- Key comeback mechanic

### Burn Economy
- Must balance playing cards vs. boosting
- Running out of DON!! limits options

**Source:** https://tcg-central.com/en/articles/how-to-play-one-piece-tcg-complete-rules-guide-2025.php

## 8. Card Abilities

### Types of Abilities
- **On Play:** Trigger when card is played
- **On Attack:** Trigger when attacking
- **On Block:** Trigger when blocking
- **On KO:** Trigger when character is KO'd
- **End of Turn:** Trigger at end phase
- **Continuous:** Always active

### DON!! Abilities
- Many cards have effects when DON!! is spent
- Triggered abilities from resource usage

### Leader Abilities
- Unique to each Leader
- Define deck strategy
- Usually powerful effects

**Source:** https://en.onepiece-cardgame.com/pdf/rule_manual.pdf

## 10. Limited Formats

### Sealed Deck
- Build from sealed product
- Usually 1 booster box
- Build 50-card minimum
- No draft portion

### Booster Draft
- Draft from 3 packs
- Pick 1 card, pass remainder
- Build 50-card minimum
- Tests deck-building skill

### Starter Deck
- Pre-constructed decks for beginners
- Good starting point
- Legal in tournament play (depending on format)

**Source:** https://en.onepiece-cardgame.com/pdf/tournament_rules_manual.pdf
