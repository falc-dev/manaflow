## 1. Game Objective & Winning Conditions

### Primary Objective
Defeat your opponent by reducing their Life to zero through strategic card play and resource management.

### Starting Conditions
- Players start with a deck of 50+ cards
- Each player has a Hero card that defines their faction and abilities
- Players start with specific Life points (varies by format)

### Victory
- Reduce opponent's Life to 0
- Alternative win conditions may exist from specific cards

### Alternative Victory
- Some cards or abilities may provide alternative victory conditions

**Source:** https://altered.gg/news/the-formats-of-altered-standard

## 2. Card Types

### Heroes
- Central character cards that define your faction
- Provide unique abilities and strategies
- Can be customized with different cards

### Characters
- Main cards used in gameplay
- Various types with different abilities
- Form the core of your deck

### Events
- One-time effect cards
- Used for tactical advantages
- Go to discard after resolution

### Locations (Stages)
- Persistent cards that affect gameplay
- Stay in play until destroyed or replaced
- Provide ongoing benefits or abilities

### Items/Equipment
- Attach to characters for bonuses
- Provide additional abilities or stats
- Can be transferred between characters

## 3. Game Zones

### Deck (Library)
- Draw pile
- Face-down
- Shuffle when required

### Hand
- Cards available to play
- No hand size limit

### Battle Area (Field)
- Where characters are placed for play
- Active cards in play

### Resource Zone
- Cards used to pay costs
- Provides resources for playing cards

### Discard Pile (Graveyard)
- Used/discarded cards go here
- Can be searched by certain effects

### Exile Zone
- Cards removed from game completely
- Cannot be recovered

### Staging Zone
- Cards waiting to enter play
- Some cards enter here first

**Source:** https://altered-prod-eu.s3.eu-west-3.amazonaws.com/downloads/comprehensive-rules/2024_Altered_TCG_Comprehensive_Rules_1.1_EN.pdf

## 4. Card rarities & Card Limits

### Card Rarities
- **Common:** Basic cards, no limit in draft
- **Uncommon:** Less frequent, important cards
- **Rare:** Powerful cards, limited in constructed
- **Unique:** Most powerful cards, very limited

### Deck Construction Limits (Standard)
- **Maximum 3 Unique cards** in deck
- **Maximum 15 Rare cards** in deck
- **Maximum 3 copies** of any card with same name
- Minimum deck size varies by format

### Factions
- Each Hero belongs to a specific faction
- Cards may be faction-specific or neutral
- Faction synergy is important for deck building

**Source:** https://altered.gg/news/the-formats-of-altered-standard

## 5. Turn Structure

### Beginning Phase
- Start of turn effects trigger
- Draw phase begins

### Draw Phase
- Draw cards from deck
- May have restrictions on number drawn

### Main Phase
- Play cards from hand
- Activate abilities
- Set up resources

### Combat Phase
- Declare attacks
- Resolve combat
- Apply damage and effects

### End Phase
- End of turn effects resolve
- Discard excess cards if hand limit exceeded
- Pass turn to opponent

## 6. Combat System

### Attack Declaration
- Choose attacking characters
- Declare targets
- Apply attack modifiers

### Defense
- Opponent can block with defenders
- Blockers assigned to attackers
- Combat resolves simultaneously

### Damage Resolution
- Apply damage to characters
- Characters may have defense/reduction
- Characters can be destroyed

### Effects
- On-damage effects trigger
- On-destruction effects trigger
- Combat-specific abilities activate

## 7. Abilities & Keywords

### Ability Types
- **Passive:** Always active
- **Activated:** Can be used when certain conditions met
- **Triggered:** Activate when specific events occur

### Common Keywords
- **Strike:** Deal damage
- **Guard:** Can block additional attackers
- **Rush:** Can attack immediately after being played
- **Lifesteal:** Gain life when dealing damage
- **Shield:** Prevents damage

### Resource System
- Cards can be used as resources
- Some cards generate resources
- Resource management is crucial

## 8. Constructed Formats

### Standard
- **Deck Size:** 50+ cards
- **Rarity Limits:** Max 3 Unique, Max 15 Rare
- **Copy Limit:** Max 3 copies per card
- Most common tournament format
- New player recommended format

### Expanded (If Available)
- Broader card pool
- Additional sets legal
- May have different restrictions

**Source:** https://altered.gg/news/the-formats-of-altered-standard

## 9. Limited Formats

### Sealed Deck
- Build from sealed product
- Build deck from cards opened
- Usually 40+ card minimum
- Great for beginners

### Booster Draft
- Draft from booster packs
- Pick cards one at a time
- Build minimum card deck
- Tests adaptability

- Swiss rounds based on player count
- Best-of matches
- Time limits enforced
